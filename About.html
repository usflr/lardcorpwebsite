History

Lard Corporation was created in 2018 by request from the former Lardish King, Tutita Col. He became the CEO, but in 2019, during the midterm elections, he was accused by his political opponents of treason and eventually resigned and his direct brother, Lard Lapudding (current President of the Lardish Republic), became the second CEO. In 2021, Tutita Col returned to the company, and he and his brother reached an agreement. The agreement established that Lard Lapudding would resign from his CEO position and would return it to his brother and the founder, Tutita Col.

Current situation

In 2021, after Tutita Col became CEO again, Lard Corporation was bought by the Lardish Government, and since then, we have improved the quality of our services.

Original Ideas and the creation

In 2020, Mr. Lard Lapudding and Tutita Col wanted to create a democratic, free and big country for all the people, and in 2021, the Lardish Republic was founded under the name of the United States of the First Lardish Republic with its capital city being the Central Lardish District. In 2022, the President of the Lardish Republic asked the Congress to add the Vicepresident and the Prime Minister position. His petition was accepted and the country's republican system changed in March 2022.

Current Political system

After the Vicepresident fleed the country, the President issued a decree making the country a presidential republic. The Prime Minister position stayed, and even after two Revolutionary wars that attempted to overturn the decision, the country nowadays remains a presidential republic.
