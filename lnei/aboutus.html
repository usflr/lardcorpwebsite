About us

We are an autonomous organization, funded by Lard Corporation, that manages all the public democratic processes in the Lardish Republic.

Our core values

HONESTY

We believe that honesty is important for humans. With honesty everyone achieves more. The LNEI publishes all of its results with full honesty.

PRIVACY

All votes are private and secret in the Lardish Republic. We ensure that no one can know who you voted for. The secret vote has been implemented since the constitutional reform of 2022 and the LNEI works to guarantee this right to all voters.

Guaranteed Results

The LNEI guarantees that all results will be available anytime, to anyone, as soon as possible. Our team works efficiently and quickly to determine the election's results and let them out to the public. 

PACIFISM

In the LNEI, we believe in conditional pacifism. We do not endorse the use of violence to take care of an issue, instead, we prefer talks. During the war between the three Lardish Republics, we served as the mediator during the peace talks that led to the ceasefire and an agreement.
