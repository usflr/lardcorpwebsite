Got questions?

Can I vote online?

Yes, all Lardish citizens have the right to request an online ballot for any election. 

How do I request the online ballot?

If you want to vote online, you will need the official online ballot.
Download and install the official Android app to vote digitally.

How do I renew my Voter's ID?

The Voter's ID is a key piece to vote in the country, if your ID expired you can schedule an appointment to renew it at our support email.

Can I vote with my DNID?

Voting with the DNID requires a previous pre-approval by your local representative of the Electoral Institute. To request this approval, visit your local LNEI offices.

If I have two citizenships, one being Lardish, can I vote? 

Only people considered Natural Lardish citizens who have two citizenships are allowed to vote. People born in another country who acquired Lardish citizenship by their own will, after living in the other country for at least 5 years is considered non-natural Lardish citizen. They are not allowed to vote, to prevent election rigging.
