Chapter IV. Introduction to the words



The development of the Lardish language was affected by some ownership changes, therefore, it only has about 70 words. Many of these words are based off English and Spanish, however there is still some Russian presence in some words. Lardish words are pronounced the same way they are written, for example you pronounce “какя” as “kakya”.
